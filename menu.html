Subscription Boxes

Each box contains a curation of the world's best coffees. Each month you'll get the best single origin coffee in the market along with brewing tips and fun facts about the country.

This selection includes coffees from: Ethiopia, Uganda, Ivory Coast, Tanzania and Kenya. A few trademark aspects that you can expect from these beans are:
  • Floral or fruity aroma
  • Full bodied
  • Rich
  • Bright acidity
  • Notes of citrus, berries and wine

This selection includes coffees from: Vietnam, and Indonesia (from multiple islands). A few trademark aspects that you can expect from these beans are:
  • Earthy/chocolate aroma
  • Smooth and full-bodied
  • Rich
  • Low acidity
  • Notes of chocolate, earth and ripe fruit

This selection includes coffees from: Guatemala, El Salvador, Honduras, Nicaragua and Costa Rica. A few trademark aspects that you can expect from these beans are:
  • Sweet aroma
  • Smooth and balanced
  • Wine-toned or fruity acidity
  • Notes of citrus, tropical fruit, and spices

This selection includes coffees from: Mexico and Hawaii. A few trademark aspects that you can expect from these beans are:
  • Floral or fruity aroma
  • Smooth or Full bodied
  • Notes of fruit, wine, chocolate, nuts and spices

This selection includes coffees from: Brazil, Colombia and Peru. A few trademark aspects that you can expect from these beans are:
  • Brazilian coffee is quite mild and can be used in blends
  • Colombian coffee is somewhat mild with a sweet aroma, medium to full body, and rich taste. Notes of fruit, nuts, chocolate and caramel-toned sweetness and citric acidity.
  • Notes of fruit, wine, chocolate, nuts and spices

This selection will includes coffees from each continent so you can experience a variety of flavor profiles.
